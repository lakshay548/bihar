Bihar

Bihar is one of the oldest inhabited places in the world, with a rich legacy in education, culture, and politics. It has Nalanda, one of the first residential universities in the world

Culture and Traditions

The folk music, Madhubani paintings, and Chhath Puja are integral parts of Bihar's cultural identity.

Popular Places to Visit

  • Mahabodhi Temple, Bodh Gaya
  • Vikramshila University
  • Rajgir Hills
